Core Program Protocol

Detailed guidelines and procedures for each segment of the event

The Narrative Catalyst

09:40 - 09:50

  • Presider Protocol: The Presider arrives 5 minutes late to simulate disorganization of the system.
  • Letter Delivery: Reading of the emotional "Rotimi's Letter" to frame the event as a response to systemic failure.
  • Agenda Transition: Presider calls the house to order and transitions to the Keynote speaker.

Keynote & Panel Sessions

  • Time Guard: Strict adherence to allotted time (40 min Keynote, 50 min Panel).
  • Q&A Protocol: Questions pre-vetted or screened live to ensure relevance to the theme.

Debate Competition

  • Audience Polling: Digital poll administered before semi-finals and after finals to measure perspective shift.
  • Judges Protocol: Judges isolated during deliberation. Chief Judge announces results with brief feedback.

Fireside Chat & Magazine Launch

  • Focus: Interviewee's role in shaping the future and value of persistent voice.
  • Launch Sequence: Introduction of editorial team, unveiling of LAUMSA Magazine, fundraising campaign announcement.

Sponsor Display & Break

13:00 - 13:30

  • Total Sponsor Time: 20 minutes allocated throughout the day.
  • Media Protocol: Gold sponsors receive 3 minutes; Silver sponsors receive 1 minute.

BP-Style Debate Protocol

British Parliamentary debate format guidelines and procedures

Pre-Event Setup

  • The Narrative Hook: Rotimi Letter reading introduces the core dilemma.
  • Moderator Briefing: Focus on systemic issues and ethical principles, not personal attacks.
  • Room Configuration: Parliamentary-style seating to encourage participation.

During Event Execution

  • Introduction: Moderator introduces the motion regarding Rotimi's advocacy.
  • Combat-Style Discussion: Facilitates two distinct sides with brief interventions.
  • Floor Management: Balancing speakers from opposing viewpoints.
  • Closing: Synthesizing arguments and linking to need for advocacy training.

Post-Event Follow-up

  • Content Extraction: Isolate powerful quotes for social media content.
  • Training Conversion: Use emotional engagement to market advocacy training programs.
